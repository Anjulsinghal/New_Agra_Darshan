Therapist

Behavioral Scientist

Social Initiatives

Jail Bandhu Project

Central Jail, Agra was a place of innumerable cathartic moments….no no it wasn’t just for the inmates but for ‘All of us team members’ who participated in the rehab and group counseling programs.

SOS Jail Chaupati

Jail Chaupati, nestled within Agra Jail, is an innovative café designed specifically for the prison community. Spearheaded by Dr. Naveen Gupta, this remarkable initiative aims to provide prisoners with an uplifting environment where they can enjoy quality food within the confines of the jail.

SOS Tiffin Services

Dr. Naveen Gupta took up the SOS Bhojanalaya platform and started this tiffin service. By profession, he is a civil engineer. SOS Tiffin Service continues its efforts toward developing better and more responsible citizens despite limited resources.

SOS Taxi Services

Lapka to Aapka is a transformative project targeting auto and taxi drivers to reshape their image and improve service standards in Agra. The project focuses on training drivers to be more hospitable, customer-centric, and socially responsible, thus changing the image of 'Lapka' (harasser) to 'Aapka' (your friendly host).

SOS Anti-Tobacco

Not only does tobacco use harm the smoker, but it also poses risks to those around them through second-hand smoke exposure. Children and non-smoking adults who are regularly exposed to second-hand smoke are at an increased risk for developing respiratory illnesses and cardiovascular disease.
